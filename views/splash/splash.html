What is Agua?

It's a platform, that helps individuals or teams manage and organize their tasks, assignments, or activities. It provides a centralized place to create, track, and prioritize tasks, ensuring that nothing important gets overlooked. To-do apps are commonly used for personal task management, project management, and collaboration purposes.

Scheduling

Scheduling, which are tasks that repeat at regular intervals. Users can set tasks to recur daily, weekly, monthly, or on custom schedules. This feature is helpful for tasks that need to be performed repeatedly, such as a project that you think how to do it or the project you finished it.
